# X Research Skills

Reusable Hermes skills for X/Twitter market research, account discovery, and content transformation.

## Included Skills

### 1. x行情观点搜索
**Folder:** `skills/x-hangqing-guandian-sousuo/`

Search X/Twitter for BTC, ETH, or narrative-specific market-analysis tweets, filter obvious noise and marketing, then output a Chinese summary of:
- main viewpoints
- representative tweets
- consensus vs disagreement
- key price levels / logic

### 2. x高质量分析账号筛选
**Folder:** `skills/x-gaozhiliang-fenxi-zhanghao-shaixuan/`

Find high-quality X/Twitter analysis accounts worth following long term around BTC, ETH, or a specific narrative. Outputs a watchlist with A/B/C tiers and account-style notes.

### 3. x推文研究转内容
**Folder:** `skills/x-tuiwen-yanjiu-zhuan-neirong/`

Turn a batch of English X/Twitter analysis tweets into Chinese research assets, including:
- research summaries
- viewpoint extraction
- short post drafts
- thread outlines
- topic ideas

## Recommended Workflow

```text
Raw X/Twitter content
        ↓
[1] x行情观点搜索
Find what the market is saying now
        ↓
[2] x高质量分析账号筛选
Find who is worth tracking long term
        ↓
[3] x推文研究转内容
Turn external content into Chinese research/content assets
```

## Who This Repo Is For

This repo is useful if you want to:
- monitor BTC / ETH market narratives on X
- build a stable watchlist of quality crypto-analysis accounts
- turn English tweet streams into usable Chinese research and content

## Notes

- These skills assume a Hermes-style skill workflow.
- The X access route described in the skills is based on browser-auth / unofficial access patterns, so stability may change over time.
- These skills are for research and content preparation, not direct trade signaling.
